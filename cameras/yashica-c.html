History

The Yashica-C improved on the A model with a four-element Yashikor lens, delivering noticeably sharper images. It represented the sweet spot in Yashica's lineup: better optics than the A without the premium of the Mat series.

Innovations

  • 4-element Yashikor lens
  • Improved build quality

Collector Notes

Mid-range model between A and D. Short production run.
