History

The 2.8E brought the wider f/2.8 aperture to photographers before built-in meters became standard. Its bright viewfinder and excellent low-light capability made it popular with available-light photographers.

Innovations

  • Wide f/2.8 aperture

Collector Notes

Added the built-in meter that became standard on the 2.8F.
