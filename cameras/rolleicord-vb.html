History

The Rolleicord Vb was the last model in this distinguished line, produced until 1977. It represents the ultimate evolution of Rollei's budget TLR concept and is prized by users seeking Rollei quality at reasonable prices.

Innovations

  • Final evolution of the Rolleicord line

Collector Notes

The ultimate Rolleicord. Excellent value compared to Rolleiflex. White face version is slightly collectible.
