History

The C330f added a focus lock to prevent accidental focus shifts — a small but crucial improvement for studio work. Combined with seven available lenses from 55mm to 250mm, it became the definitive professional TLR system.

Notable Photographers

Studio and portrait photographers who needed the security of locked focus.

Innovations

  • Focus lock mechanism

Collector Notes

Refined C330. Most popular professional TLR. Extremely versatile system camera.
