History

The original Mamiyaflex C, introduced in 1956, established the interchangeable-lens TLR concept that would define Mamiya's medium format legacy. It was the foundation for a system that would serve professional photographers for nearly four decades.

Innovations

  • Interchangeable lens TLR concept

Collector Notes

Historic first interchangeable lens TLR. Foundation for all later C models.
