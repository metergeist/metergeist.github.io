History

The C330S was the last camera in Mamiya's legendary TLR line, produced until 1994. It incorporated all lessons learned from decades of professional feedback, representing the ultimate expression of the interchangeable-lens TLR concept.

Cultural Significance

Produced until 1994, outlasting nearly all other TLR production.

Innovations

  • Final refinement of C-series

Collector Notes

Last and most refined C330. The ultimate Mamiya TLR. Discontinued 1994.
