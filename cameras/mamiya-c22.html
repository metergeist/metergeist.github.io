Mamiya C22 Professional

Streamlined design for professional workflows.

1966–1968 6x6 on 120/220 film Made in Tokyo, Japan

Photo: DANYvanvee at English Wikipedia · Public domain · Source

Specifications

Taking LensInterchangeable (55mm-250mm available)
Viewing LensMatched to taking lens
Lens ElementsVaries by lens
ShutterSeiko leaf shutter (in lens)
Shutter Speeds1s - 1/500s + B
Light MeterNone
Filter MountBay or filter thread (varies)
FocusingBellows
  • Lighter than C33
  • Manual shutter cocking
  • First to fully support 220 film
  • Simpler mechanism

History

The C22 offered a more streamlined approach to the professional TLR, with refined controls designed for faster operation in demanding professional environments.

Collector Notes

Budget alternative to C33. Predecessor to C220.
