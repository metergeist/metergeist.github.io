History

The 3.5E models preceded the metered F series, offering the same legendary optics in a simpler, meter-free design. Many photographers prefer these for their mechanical purity and the fact that there's no meter to fail.

Innovations

  • Refined pre-meter design

Collector Notes

Added built-in metering to the 3.5 line.
